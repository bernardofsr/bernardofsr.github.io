About This Blog

[ 2026-05-13 ]

This blog is a personal space dedicated to cybersecurity, technology and everything surrounding the field.

The main objective is simple: to document ideas, notes, research, experiments and lessons learned throughout the journey in cybersecurity.

Some posts may focus on operational security, threat hunting, detection engineering or offensive security. Others may simply be reflections about tools, methodologies or interesting concepts discovered along the way.

Cybersecurity is an area that evolves constantly. There is always something new to learn, break, analyze or understand better.

This blog is less about appearing as an expert and more about documenting growth, curiosity and continuous learning.

Over time, this space will become a public archive of the path taken in the field — a collection of notes, projects, mistakes, ideas and technical observations.

If something here becomes useful to someone else, even better.
