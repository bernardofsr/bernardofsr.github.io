Bernardo Rodrigues

Cybersecurity Auditor / Ethical Hacker from Portugal.

My name is Bernardo Rodrigues, I'm 23 years old and I'm from Portugal. From a very early age I had contact with the computer world and the taste has grown over the years.

During my teens I came across the term hacking and it was a one-way street. In my secondary education I attended a professional course in computer equipment management with more involvement in the hardware part and in higher education I attended the course in Technologies and Information System Programming at Coimbra Institute of Engineering.

I currently work at Cipher's Red Team Services, where I started since February 2021, this being my first job in the area.

Also as "part-time" I usually join some Bug Bounty programs on HackerOne platform, where I have completed reports for companies like Sony, US Department of Defense (DoD), BMW Group, Ford Group, Defense Industrial Base by DoD, among others.
